How to Play Käsekästchen

  1. Start the game: Click on the 'Start' button.
  2. Draw a line: On your turn, click on a dot to start a line and click on an adjacent dot to finish the line.
  3. Complete a box: If you complete a box, you get another turn. The box will be filled with your color.
  4. End of the game: The game ends when all boxes have been filled. The player with the most boxes wins.

Good luck and have fun!
