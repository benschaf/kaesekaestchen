How to Play Käsekästchen

  1. Draw a line: On your turn, click on a line to draw it.
  2. Complete boxes: Once a player draws the fourth line around a box, the box is completed. The box will be filled with your color. If you complete a box, you get another turn.
  3. End of the game: The game ends when all boxes have been filled. The player who filled the most boxes wins.
  4. Play again: Try changing some options in the options panel this time. Then click "Start new Game"

Good luck and have fun!
