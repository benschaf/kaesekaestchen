Käsekästchen is a simple game, where you draw lines to fill boxes. The player who fills the most boxes wins.

Click on How to Play to learn the rules or start a new game right away by drawing the first border. An AI player will play against you automatically.

How to Play Käsekästchen

  1. Draw a line: On your turn, click on the border of a box to draw it.
  2. Complete boxes: Once a player draws the fourth line around a box, the box is completed. The box will be filled with your color. If you complete a box, you get another turn.

    You can also use the lines drawn by the AI to your advantage. If the AI draws a line, you can use it to complete a box on your next turn.

  3. End of the game: The game ends when all boxes have been filled. The player who filled the most boxes wins.
  4. Play again: Try changing some options in the options panel this time. Then click "Restart Game"

Good luck and have fun!
